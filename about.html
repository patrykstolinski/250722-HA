Welcome to Xavier's School for gifted Children

Welcome to Xavier’s School for Gifted Youngsters — a safe haven and training ground for the next generation of mutants. Founded by Professor Charles Xavier, our mission is simple: to guide young mutants in mastering their unique abilities, to prepare them for a world that may not yet understand them, and to show them that difference is not a weakness — it's power.

Located in a secluded estate in Westchester County, the school is more than just an educational facility. It’s a community, a sanctuary, and a launchpad for heroes. Our students come from all walks of life, united by one thing: the mutant X-gene.

Whether you're learning to control optic blasts, manipulate weather, or simply figure out who you are, Xavier’s offers a place to grow, learn, and become something greater.
